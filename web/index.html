    About SignalPulse AI

    Built for 22nd Century Technologies delivery teams

    What it is

    SignalPulse AI helps teams stay current on public U.S. cyber and regulatory updates without chasing websites by hand. It collects data from official sources on a schedule (or when you run a collection), stores that material in one place, and lets people ask questions in plain English. Answers point back to the original documents.

    Data Factory

    Keep the corpus current

    Collectors pull data from sources such as CISA, NVD, the Federal Register, NIST, HealthIT.gov, and NASCIO. Text is cleaned, organized, and stored so it can be searched later. Unchanged documents are skipped on the next run.

    Use Intelligence Hub, Corpus, and Data Factory to see what is already loaded.

    Ask Assistant

    Get cited answers

    Open Ask Assistant and type a question. The system finds relevant passages in the stored corpus and answers from that material, with links you can open. If the corpus does not cover the question, it says so instead of guessing.

    Sources in scope

    • Cyber: CISA known exploited vulnerabilities, NVD CVE details
    • Federal Register: CMS, HHS/ONC, and Defense notices
    • Standards: NIST news, CSF, RMF, SP 800-53
    • Health IT: HealthIT.gov / ONC updates
    • State: NASCIO CIO priorities

    Public government information only. Not confidential company data.

    Good to know

    • This is an internal demo and evaluation prototype.
    • It is not legal or compliance advice.
    • Collection is scheduled or on demand, not live second by second.
    • For important decisions, confirm details on the original .gov page.
